How can we bring back creativity and cultural excitement to an area
already so legendary for its rich musical heritage?

The Farsight Collective are a team of experienced, independent operators, proposing an extraordinary new community arts, event and grassroots music venue for St Giles - with an outstanding training and cultural program.

Please join us at our community information sessions, where we’ll showcase the design plan, the licensing strategy, public realm improvement and culture proposals including stakeholder outreach strategies to attendees. Each session will begin with a short presentation followed by a Q & A session with key figures in the Collective.
